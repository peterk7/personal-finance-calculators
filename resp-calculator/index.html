RESP & CESG Rules Reference

Lifetime Limit
$50,000

Maximum total contributions per beneficiary. No annual contribution limit, but exceeding the lifetime cap incurs penalties.

CESG Match
20%

The government matches 20% of the first $2,500 contributed each year — that's $500/year in free money.

CESG Lifetime Cap
$7,200

Maximum total CESG a beneficiary can receive. At $500/year, this is reached in about 14.4 years.

Catch-Up Rule
$5,000/yr

If you missed years, contribute up to $5,000/year to claim up to $1,000 CESG/year on accumulated unused room.

For educational purposes only. This calculator uses simplified assumptions. Consult a financial advisor for personalized advice. CESG eligibility requires a valid SIN and the beneficiary must be under 18.
